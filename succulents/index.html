Before You Browse

How we describe size and form

Every variety page states its actual crown width in cm and inches — we don't use generic S/M/L/XL buckets, since growth habit varies a lot between forms. Here's what the growth-form labels mean:

Plug-tray startYoung, compact size grown from cell trays — smallest available size.
Standard / single rosetteOne mature rosette, our most common size for retail bundles.
Multi-head clusterSeveral rosettes grown together on one root system.
Old-stalk specimenMature, branching, multi-headed plant developed over multiple growing seasons.
Crested (cristate)A naturally occurring mutation where the growing point fans into a wavy ridge.
Potted starterAlready established in a growing container, ready for retail display.
