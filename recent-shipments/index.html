Before your carton ever reaches a courier, we document it — weighed, sealed, and certified. Here's exactly what that process looks like, and how to get reference photos from a recent shipment to your region.

Our pre-dispatch documentation process

Every wholesale order gets the same four-step verification before it leaves Yunnan — no exceptions, no shortcuts.

Step 1

Pack & seal

Each plant wrapped and nested in the export carton per our standard packing protocol.

Step 2

Weigh on camera

The sealed carton is photographed on the scale — the exact weight is sent to you before dispatch.

Step 3

Certify

Phytosanitary certificate, commercial invoice, and packing list are attached and photographed.

Step 4

Share tracking

Your DHL/FedEx tracking number is sent the same day the carton is collected.
