Care & Acclimation

Care Basics

  • Light: Bright, indirect light or morning sun. Full afternoon summer sun can scorch leaves — dark cultivars hold colour best with partial shade in hot climates.
  • Water: Water when soil is fully dry. Aeonium grow actively in cooler months and may go semi-dormant in peak summer heat.
  • Propagation: Stem cuttings root readily — let the wound callous for 2–3 days, then place in well-draining mix.

Packing Details

Each plant is individually wrapped in breathable cotton paper, air-dried for 2–4 days before packing to reduce transit moisture, and nested in custom shredded packaging inside a ventilated export carton.

Shipping & Documents

Every shipment includes an official Phytosanitary Certificate (GACC-issued), Commercial Invoice, and Packing List. Air-freight transit generally takes 7–12 days door to door.
