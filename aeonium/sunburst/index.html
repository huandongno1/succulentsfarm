Aeonium 'Sunburst' — Large Tricolour Rosettes

Aeonium 'Sunburst' is one of the most widely recognised variegated Aeonium cultivars — bold cream, yellow, and pink rosettes reaching 30–40cm across. SouthQuest Farm grows comparable large-form tricolour hybrids in Yunnan, China, at 2,000m elevation for wholesale export.

About This Cultivar

'Sunburst' developed as a large-form variegated sport, producing dinner-plate-scale rosettes with distinct colour banding — pink margins fading through cream to a yellow-green centre. Its size and bold colouration have made it a staple of premium succulent retail displays worldwide.

Care Basics

  • Light: Bright, direct-to-indirect light; strong light intensifies the pink edge colouring
  • Water: Soak-and-dry; more active growth in cooler months
  • Propagation: Stem cuttings; variegated sections can occasionally revert to solid green and should be pruned out
  • Common issue: Sunburn on the thinner variegated leaf tissue in intense, unfiltered summer sun
Sourcing note: 'Sunburst' itself is not a fixed line in our current catalogue. We grow comparable large-form tricolour hybrids such as 'Autumn Hues' — share a reference photo and we'll confirm the closest match in stock.
