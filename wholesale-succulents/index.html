Logistics

All wholesale shipments move by air freight via DHL Express or FedEx International Priority, with real-time tracking. Typical transit times:

  • North America: 5–9 business days
  • Europe: 5–8 business days
  • Australia: 8–14 business days
  • Asia-Pacific: 3–6 business days

Every shipment includes a Phytosanitary Certificate (GACC-issued), Commercial Invoice with correct HS tariff codes, and a detailed Packing List — sent electronically 24–48 hours before physical arrival.

Dead-on-Arrival Guarantee

We photograph every plant before it leaves our facility. If the dead-or-unsellable rate exceeds 3% of a shipment (for example, more than 3 units in a 100-plant order):

  1. Send photos of the affected plants within 48 hours of delivery
  2. We verify against our pre-shipment records
  3. Affected units are credited or replaced on your next order
🛡️ Our pre-shipment drying and cotton-wrap packing protocol is built to keep plants healthy on transit routes of 14+ days.

Common Questions

Wholesale ordering — what buyers ask

Our standard MOQ is 50 plants per shipment for mixed-variety retail and e-commerce orders. Nursery and landscape wholesale programs typically start at 500 pieces to optimise the per-unit air-freight cost. Collector orders are a separate, smaller category — 10+ hand-selected specimens per shipment, independent of the standard 50-plant MOQ.
Unit price depends on size tier, from plug-tray starts up to exhibition-grade old-stalk specimens. We quote a per-variety, per-size price sheet once you share your target list and quantities — there is no single flat price across all 117 varieties.
We photograph every shipment before it leaves our facility. If the dead-or-unsellable rate exceeds 3% of the shipment (for example, more than 3 units in a 100-plant order), send photos within 48 hours of delivery and we will credit or replace the affected units on your next order.
Orders from current stock ship within 3–7 business days of confirmed payment, then air-freight transit adds another 5–10 business days depending on destination — so total lead time from RFQ to delivery is usually 2–3 weeks combining both stages.
